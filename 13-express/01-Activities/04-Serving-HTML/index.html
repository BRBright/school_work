About Me

My name is Billy Joe Ray Bright. I am 26 years old and live in Sacramento Ca. I have lived in Illinois for two year with my father. While there I learned all of my work ethic. After living there with my father I moved back Cali to live with my mother where I enrolled and finished my jr. high school and high school career at Foot Hill High School.

After graduation I enrolled in American River Collage. My major at the time was Commercial Music and Recording. I really liked the first few levels of the major but shortly found out that it was not for me. After figuring out that major was not me I got a job at Dairy Queen. While there I was taught how the work place is and customer service. I spent four years there starting from the bottom working my way up to Shift lead.

While working at Dairy Queen for four year it was time to move on. After I left there I worked for Bed Bath and Beyond for a short time before I figured out that retail was not my cup of tea. So after figuring that out I got a job at Amazon. I had applied on a whim not really expecting to really get the job. But after a few days I got an email back telling me to got to new hire event. Then a few months later in October 2017 the building opened. They hired something like 4000 people all at the same time plus it was also the begnning of the holiday season.
